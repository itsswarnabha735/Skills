# M&A Target Evaluation - Sentinel

## Purpose

Structured evaluation of an acquisition target using the MAP protocol.
Forces independent scoring across 6 dimensions before synthesis.
Designed for a single evaluator or as the individual sheet in a
committee noise audit.

Run this BEFORE:
- Making a recommendation to a board or investment committee
- Signing an LOI or term sheet
- Authorizing significant additional due diligence spend

## Framing check (answer before scoring)

**Q1 - Sunk cost test (MA1)**:
If this deal appeared on your desk today with no prior work done -
no relationship, no data room, no management presentations - would
you pursue it at the current price and terms?
[ ] Yes, clearly  [ ] Probably yes  [ ] Uncertain  [ ] Probably not  [ ] No

*If uncertain or no: your prior engagement may be distorting your evaluation.*

**Q2 - Outside view check (MA5)**:
What is the success rate of comparable deals in this sector,
deal type, and size range?
Success rate (from external data): ___%

*If you don't have this number - consult BASE_RATES_MA before scoring.*

**Q3 - Competition signal (MA8)**:
How many other parties reviewed this deal?
Number of other bidders: ___
*If you are winning a competitive process: your price is evidence you estimated higher than all others. Adjust accordingly.*

## Questioner - the questions you'd skip

*Answer in writing before making a recommendation.*

**Q1 - The fatal flaw question**:
What is the one thing that, if true, would make you walk away from this deal?
Is there evidence this thing is true?

**Q2 - The dissent question**:
Who on the deal team has expressed reservations about this deal?
What specifically did they say? Were they heard or managed?

**Q3 - The integration question**:
Name the person who will run the integration on day 1 post-close.
What is their track record with integrations of this complexity?
