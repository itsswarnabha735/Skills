## Score/Confidence pattern

- **High score + high confidence** -> Green light
- **High score + low confidence** -> Potential but uncertain - test first
- **Low score + high confidence** -> Real weakness - improve or abandon
- **Low score + low confidence** -> Not enough info - investigate
