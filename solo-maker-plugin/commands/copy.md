## Copy Surfaces

Detect which type(s) are needed and execute:

### Landing Page Copy
Full copy pack:
- 5 headline options (different formulas)
- 3 sub-headline options
- Problem section copy
- Solution intro
- Feature descriptions (reframed as benefits)
- 5 CTA button options
- 3 testimonial templates (with realistic specifics)
- FAQ answers for top 5 objections

### Email Sequence (5-email welcome/onboarding)
1. **Welcome** — deliver promised lead magnet + set expectations
2. **Value email** — teach one useful thing (no sell)
3. **Story** — founder journey, why this matters
4. **Proof** — case study or testimonial in story form
5. **Offer** — soft pitch with clear CTA

Each email: Subject line + 3 variations + full body

### Social Media Bio
- Twitter/X (160 chars)
- LinkedIn headline + about section
- Instagram bio
- TikTok bio

### Ad Copy
3 variations for:
- Facebook/Instagram (awareness + conversion)
- Google Search (headline + description)
- LinkedIn (if B2B)

### Pitch / Sales Script
- 30-second elevator pitch
- 2-minute verbal pitch
- Cold email outreach (3 variations)

## Deliver

Output as `[product]-copy-pack.md` with clear sections.
