# Standard Product Demo Narrative Arc

This template provides a standard 5-part structure for a compelling product demo video script.

---

### 1. The Problem (The "Before")

-   **Goal:** Hook the viewer by describing a relatable problem or pain point that they experience.
-   **Content:**
    -   Start with the status quo.
    -   Show the frustration, inefficiency, or challenge.
    -   Use language that resonates with the target audience (from the ICP).
-   **Example Narration:** "Tired of juggling multiple spreadsheets just to track your sales leads? It's chaotic, and things inevitably fall through the cracks."

### 2. The Solution (The "After")

-   **Goal:** Introduce your product as the elegant solution to the problem.
-   **Content:**
    -   Present your product's name and core value proposition.
    -   Show a clean, simple shot of the main dashboard or interface.
    -   This is the "aha!" moment.
-   **Example Narration:** "Introducing SoloCRM. The single source of truth for your entire sales process, designed for solopreneurs."

### 3. How It Works (The "Magic")

-   **Goal:** Demonstrate the 2-3 key features that solve the core problem. Do not show every feature.
-   **Content:**
    -   Walk through a specific, high-value user flow.
    -   Focus on the benefits, not just the features.
    -   Show the "magic" â€“ the part that makes the user's life demonstrably better.
-   **Example Narration:** "Watch how easy it is to add a new lead. With one click, their data is enriched, and a follow-up task is automatically scheduled. No more manual entry."

### 4. The Differentiator

-   **Goal:** Briefly highlight what makes your solution unique.
-   **Content:**
    -   Showcase one specific feature or benefit that competitors don't have.
    -   Keep it short and impactful.
-   **Example Narration:** "And unlike other CRMs, SoloCRM's AI-powered weekly review gives you a prioritized action plan every Monday morning."

### 5. The Call to Action (CTA)

-   **Goal:** Tell the viewer exactly what to do next.
-   **Content:**
    -   Be direct and clear.
    -   Provide a URL or a next step.
-   **Example Narration:** "Stop juggling spreadsheets and start closing deals. Get started with your free trial at SoloCRM.com today."
