# Voiceover Style Guide

How to match the narration to your brand DNA.

## 1. The "Trusted Advisor" (Low Energy, High Trust)

- **Tone**: Calm, deliberate, professional.
- **Pitch**: Lowered.
- **Use for**: B2B strategy, financial reports, PRD walk-throughs.

## 2. The "Hype-Man" (High Energy, High Urgency)

- **Tone**: Exciting, fast-paced, rhythmic.
- **Pitch**: Varied.
- **Use for**: Ad scripts, social media teasers, new feature launches.

## 3. The "Helpful Peer" (Medium Energy, Conversational)

- **Tone**: Friendly, accessible, empathetic.
- **Use for**: Tutorial videos, onboarding, and casual "Build-in-public" updates.
